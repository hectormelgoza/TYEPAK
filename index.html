Why Choose TyePAK™ Over Tyvek®?

Tyepak™ surpasses Tyvek® with its advanced technology. It offers superior vapor permeability, allowing moisture to escape, while remaining liquid impermeable. This unique combination ensures durability and optimal protection, making TyePak™ the top choice for those seeking optimal protection and breathability.

Why Choose TyePAK™?

If you're transitioning from your current material and need an equivalent substitute, this liquid-impermeable solution is essential. It offers the familiar adaptability and efficiency, especially when demanding complete liquid resistance in your applications.

Vapor Permeability

Why Choose TyePAK™?

Vapor permeability assesses how effectively a material allows the passage of moisture vapor. It influences the material's applicability in diverse contexts, impacting moisture control, insulation, and protective barriers, making it a key consideration in various industries.
